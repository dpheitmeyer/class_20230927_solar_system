Sun

Our sun is the only star in our solar system. Nuclear fusion that occurs in the sun is the source of light and heat that is vital for life on earth as we know it.

  • Length of day: 24.0 hours

Earth is the fifth largest planet in our solar system. Oceans cover 70% of the Earth's surface. Earth's atmosphere is mostly nitrogen with significant amounts of oxygen.
